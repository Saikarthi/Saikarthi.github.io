Legzoid

Color is Endless game developed after Slide Run. This game is unique among other Endless games. There are three colored tracks (Green, Red, Black) and three colored obstacles (Green, Red, Black).

The ball color will change for five seconds once. The thing is this game have decreasing point pattern, which means initially certain point is given to the player and according to the player's activities in this game will decrease the point.

There is also an increase in point if the player follow certain rules. Initially the player is provided with 1000 points.

The a color ball strikes in same colored obstacle, 100 points will be reduced. If different color ball strikes in different colored obstacle, 500 points will be reduced.

If the ball is in the same colored track, once for 5 seconds the points will increase by 10. Or else the points will decrease gradually.
